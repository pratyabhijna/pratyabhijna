Please note that this study:

Has no right or wrong answers. In all the tasks, we will try to find a way for you to be able to communicate how you imagine things inside your ‘head’. There is no right or wrong answer in this, this study is only looking for descriptions and understanding of the experience of imagination.

This study does not require headphones. No audio will be played during any of these tasks.

Please do the study when you think you will be undisturbed for 15 minutes. The study has 6 tasks which take approximately 2 minutes each. You can pause the study after each task.

Please do the study in a relatively quiet and noiseless area, if possible

Each task asks you to imagine a different scenario. While imagining any scenario in each of the tasks, please keep your eyes closed. And make sure you are focused on the task.
